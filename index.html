Meet the team

Mad Pulpo is a collective of graduates from Ironhack School Barcelona, a web development bootcamp, forming the next generation of web developers.

The main goal after graduating has always been to continue learning and deepening this new knowledge. Mad Pulpo provides a platform for only the most talented, creative and ambitious graduates to learn on real-life projects from simple landing pages to complex web-apps.

By applying industry standard best practices and working with a network of experienced professionals to oversee all projects, each product is developed in the highest quality.
